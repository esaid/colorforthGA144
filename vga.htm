Putting socket in VGA site

Two problems: The hole pattern is rotated 180 degrees. The rows are too widely spaced.

I used a "HiDen 15pin female straight pcb mount" D-sub socket. Spread pins on socket.

Vertical sync is not used. Sync is composite (horizontal) sync.
